The Wix Bookings seam — what's ours vs what's panel-styled Wix on the Book page

Frame is oursinternals are panel-customized Wix.

On the Book page (P·10), the booking widget area is a static mock of how the panel-customized Wix Bookings widget will render in the production build. The frame is fully ours — typographic hero, tide line, sidebar, Velo service-area gate, what-happens-next strip. The grid internals are rendered by Wix Bookings and styled via the Studio panel: fonts, colors, button labels, divider style, show/hide of subtitles and badges. The widget's checkout button becomes our copper CTA via panel. The calendar grid layout itself is not CSS-addressable — we accept Wix-native there and design around it. See WIX_RECON.md §3 for the customization envelope.
