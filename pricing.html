§ 08 · Ask
Do you take HSA or FSA cards?
Not directly. I can produce a superbill at session end that most HSA/FSA administrators reimburse on the back end. If your provider needs a specific code, ask before the first session.
How early should I arrive?
Five minutes before is plenty. The intake is online, you don't fill paper here. The room will be ready, the music will already be on.
What's the cancellation policy?
Twenty-four hours, free cancel, full deposit refund. Inside twenty-four, fifty percent of the session fee is charged to the card on file. No-show is full fee. Late arrivals over fifteen minutes finish on time at full charge — same-week rebook with no late penalty.
Do you tip?
There's a prompt at session end if you want to. Skipping it changes nothing about how I treat you next time. The fee is the fee.
What if I'm pregnant or recovering from surgery?
Mention it on the intake. A note about timeline and your provider's clearance lets me adjust positioning, pressure, and modality. Some sessions need owner pre-review before they auto-confirm — that's by design.
