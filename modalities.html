§ 42 · Practice

Five hands-on practices. One body. Yours.

Different work, same hands. The modality is a starting point — most sessions blend across two or three depending on what the body is asking for. If you're not sure which to choose, book a 90-minute Integrated and we'll decide together on the table.

§ 43 · Choose
Start hereif it's your first session.

For a first session, almost everyone wants Deep Tissue at 90 minutes — that's the practice's most-booked default and gives the body enough runway to settle. Pregnant past the first trimester? Book Prenatal instead. Recovering from surgery, treating an oedema, or going through cancer treatment? Lymphatic, with provider clearance. Athlete in season or training for an event? Sports. Already a regular and your body is shifting? Integrated, and we'll decide on the table.

Pick a practice. Or pick the body and let the practice follow.
