§ 27 · FAQ
Do gift cards expire?
No. They're good until redeemed. If the studio ever closes, outstanding balances refund automatically.
Can the recipient use it toward a package or membership?
Yes. Apply the gift balance toward any package or the first month of membership at checkout.
Can I refund a gift card?
Within 30 days of purchase, full refund if unredeemed. After 30, the balance stays with the recipient.
What if the recipient lives out of town?
Tell them to call when they're visiting Eugene/Springfield — Lane County or close. The card doesn't transfer to other practitioners.
Can I write a personal note?
Yes. There's a note field at checkout — printed on the card or rendered in the email, your choice.
